English uses a Subject–Verb–Object Model (SOV).

For example:

`Joan cleaned her room`

**Joan** is the subject, **cleaned** is the verb and **room** is the object.

`She sang a song.`

**She** is the subject, **sang** is the verb and **song** is the object.
